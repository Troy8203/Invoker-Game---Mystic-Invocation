Instructions

The game is simple, you have to invoke the spells of the invoker hero from the game Dota 2. You have to press the keys Q, W, E to invoke the spells and D, F to cast them. The R key is used to invoke the ultimate spell. The game will end when you invoke the wrong spell or take too long to invoke the spell.

To invoke a spell, you must combine the three elemental orbs associated with the Q, W, and E keys. Each combination of orbs corresponds to a specific spell.

Your score will increase with each correctly invoked spell. The faster you invoke and cast the correct spell, the higher your score will be. If you make a mistake or take too long, the game ends, and your final score will be displayed.
